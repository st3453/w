Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

ASWClassFinder is a public directory. We collect information that you voluntarily provide when posting an advertisement, such as:

  • Your Name or Tutor Name
  • Contact details (Phone number, Email)
  • Class details (Subject, Grade, Location)
  • Images/Posters you upload

2. How We Use Your Data

The information you provide is used solely to display your advertisement to potential students. We do not sell your personal data to third-party marketing companies.

Note: Since this is a public directory, any phone number or email you include in your ad will be visible to everyone visiting the site.

3. Cookies & Analytics

We may use basic cookies to ensure the website functions correctly and to understand how many people are visiting our pages. These do not track your personal identity.

4. Data Deletion

If you have posted an advertisement and wish to have it removed from our platform, please contact us via WhatsApp. We will remove your data from our database as soon as possible.

5. Changes to This Policy

As ASWClassFinder is a new project, we may update this policy occasionally to reflect new features. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
