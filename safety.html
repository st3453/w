Stay Safe on ASWClassFinder

Your safety is our priority. Follow these simple tips when contacting teachers.

🛡️

Verify Credentials

Before making any payments, always verify the teacher's qualifications and teaching experience.

👥

Meet in Public

If you are attending a physical class, ensure it is in a recognized institute or a public learning space.

💸

Avoid Large Upfront Payments

Never pay for a full year of classes in advance to an individual. Pay monthly or per session.

🚩

Report Suspicious Ads

If an ad looks too good to be true or seems like a scam, report it to us immediately via our support email.
